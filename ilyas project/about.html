Hospital History and Achievements

Our History

Our hospital was founded in 1950 by a group of dedicated healthcare professionals who shared a vision of providing high-quality medical care to our community.

Over the years, our hospital has grown and evolved to meet the changing needs of our patients. We have invested in state-of-the-art technology and equipment, and have recruited a team of highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals.

Today, our hospital is a leading provider of healthcare services in our region, and we are proud of our reputation for excellence and compassion.

Our Achievements

  • Recipient of the "Best Hospital" award from the state health department (2010, 2015, 2020)
  • Accredited by the Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organizations (JCAHO)
  • Designated as a "Center of Excellence" for cardiovascular care by the American Heart Association
  • Recipient of the "Patient Safety Award" from the National Patient Safety Foundation (2012, 2018)
